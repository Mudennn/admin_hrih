Question

What is Probation?

Answer

An employee is put on trial to prove his/her fitness for the job position for which he/she is employed in terms of his/her performance, conduct, and character.

Explanation

  • There is no statutory definition of what a “probation” is.
  • However, case law does distinguish between a confirmed employee and a probationer.

Question

Is probation or probationary period is mandatory?

Answer

  • There is no any Legal definition of a ‘Probationer’ or ‘Probationary Period’.
  • The law does not mandate a probation.

Explanation

  • There is no any clause in the Employment Act 1955 does it say anything about probation.
  • Probation isn’t even a legal requirement.
  • Employer can ‘confirm’ an employee straight away.
